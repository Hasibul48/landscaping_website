Who We Are

More Than a Landscaping Company — A Vision

GreenScape was born from a simple but profound belief: that every outdoor space holds untapped potential for beauty, harmony, and human connection. Founded in 2013 by master landscaper Daniel Ashworth, we set out to redefine what luxury landscaping could mean.

Today, our team of 50+ dedicated designers, horticulturists, and craftsmen operate across the country, delivering bespoke landscapes that earn international recognition—and more importantly, lifelong client relationships.

What Guides Us

The Principles Behind Every Project

01

Uncompromising Quality

We use only the finest materials and plants, sourced ethically and selected for longevity. There are no shortcuts—only standards.

02

Timeless Design

We design with decades in mind. Trends come and go; our landscapes deepen in character and beauty with every passing season.

03

Ecological Responsibility

Every design integrates biodiversity, water efficiency, and native plantings. Beautiful landscapes and healthy ecosystems are not mutually exclusive.

04

Client Partnership

We don't design for you—we design with you. Deep listening and genuine collaboration produce results that feel personal, never generic.

05

Artisanal Craft

Every stone laid, every plant positioned is the result of hands trained over years. Machinery aids us; craftsmanship defines us.

06

Living Legacies

A landscape is never truly finished—it's alive and evolving. We design spaces that grow richer over time, becoming part of your family's story.
